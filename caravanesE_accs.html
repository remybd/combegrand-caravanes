How come in Combegrand ?

The address :
Combegrand
Fonters du Razès
11400 Castelnaudary

Leave the motorway A61, exit Castelnaudary. Take the D6 in direction of Mirepoix, through the village of Fendeille and after ten kilometers, you will find the the access to the farm "Combegrand" on your left.
